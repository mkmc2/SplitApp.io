Modernizing Raffles

Split offers a completely new and exciting way to raffle. Our innovative app allows you to contribute to charity, and win real money, all from your mobile device.

Coming 2019!!!
Sorry, our application is not available for download. Please get on our mailing list so we can notify you know when Split is available in your state!

1. Find Raffles

Search through the network of open Splits and find a couple you are feeling lucky about. Using geolocation, the app will automatically find raffles that are close by - helping support your local charities.

2. Enter Raffles

Once you have found a Split supporting a cause you are passionate about, entering has never been easier. Simply click the "Tap to Join" to purchase your entry.

3. Win Raffles

If you are a lucky winner, your winnings will be automatically deposited to your Split account. If not, at least your money went to a good cause! It's really a win-win.
