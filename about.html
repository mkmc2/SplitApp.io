About Us

Raffle = Split

At Split, we believe that raffles are outdated and boring. In fact, were so passionate about revolutionizing raffles, we decided to change the name. Even though a 'raffle' and a 'Split' are technically the same thing, since we're updating so much already, we thought a name chance was appropriate.

The entire process behind raffles hasn't been updated until now. We have developed a new innovative mobile application that is aimed at making raffles more exciting, beneficial, and seamless for everyone involved. Our app will change the raffle game forever.

How it works:

Traditional raffle methods include in-person cash transactions to purchase a ticket that hasn't changed since elementry school. Next, the winner is selected based off a complicated number sequence. Lastly, the lucky winner is mailed a check... I mean come on - who even uses checks these days? Split takes the same concepts of a raffle and converts them to use technology making the process much more efficient, simple, and fun.

Our mobile application allows users to join raffles as easily as texting "you up?". Your Split account is linked with your bank account so you can purchase entry without putting down your gluten-free bean-burger. All available raffles Splits are displayed directly on the applicaiton. Simply search through them all to find one (or more) you feel good about, and click "Join Split".

Instead of hovering around waiting for the winning ticket announcement, our app shows real-time stats that include total pot, other participants, and time remaining. Once that timer strikes 0, the winner will automatically be electronically sent the funds (since were in the 21st century, we don't believe in checks). Repeat this process.
